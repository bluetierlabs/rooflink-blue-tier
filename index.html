Field verification checklist

Before install, verify the current official documents.

  • Manufacturer and product line match the material on site.
  • Installation instruction date/version is current.
  • Warranty requirements are checked before work starts.
  • Deck, underlayment, ventilation, flashing, fastening, and weather limits are reviewed.
  • Local code, project specs, and safety requirements are verified separately.
  • Crew has a job packet or QR link to the official documents.
